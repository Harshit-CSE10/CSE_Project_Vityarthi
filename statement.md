# Project Statement

## Problem Statement
Small businesses often lose revenue due to poor stock tracking, resulting in overstocking or stockouts. They lack a digital system to track inventory and sales in real-time.

## Scope
A CLI application to manage product details, track stock levels, and alert users when items are low.

## Target Users
Retail shop owners and warehouse managers.

## High-Level Features
1. CRUD operations for Inventory.
2. Real-time Sales Tracking.
3. Automated Low-Stock Alerts.
